DRÖMNU

Press kit

Now based in Barcelona, DRÖMNU was born in Armenia, where he was classically trained on the violin. His music is heavily inspired by film soundtracks, Armenian melodies, and contrasted with hard-hitting percussion pulled from his hip-hop background.

His work has been released on vinyl and digitally on notable labels like Lapsus Records and Skrillex's OWSLA Records. He has performed at festivals like Sónar and MIRA, and participated in the Red Bull Music Academy Bass Camp.

DRÖMNU’s latest project is a set of 11 tracks composed dawless, performed live recreating the same setup from the studio.
