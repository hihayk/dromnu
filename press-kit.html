Now Based in Barcelona, DRÖMNU was born in Armenia, where he was classically trained with the violin. His music is heavily inspired by film soundtracks, Armenian melodies and contrasted with hard hitting percussion pulled from his hiphop background.

His work has been released on vinyls and digitally on notable labels like Lapsus Records and Skrillex's OWSLA Records. He has performed in festivals like Sónar and MIRA, and participated in the Red Bull Music Academy Bass Camp.

DRÖMNU’s latest project is a set of 11 tracks composed dawless with few instruments, which he plays live with the same studio setup.
